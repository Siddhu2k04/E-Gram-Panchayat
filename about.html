Our Vision

The E-Gram Panchayat Anurabad envisions a transparent, digitally connected village where every citizen can access information and services at their fingertips.

Our Mission

✅ To provide real-time updates on village developments
✅ To ensure open access to public notices, records, and documents
✅ To bridge the gap between the Gram Panchayat and citizens via digital tools
✅ To encourage participatory, inclusive rural governance

About Anurabad Grampanchayat

The Grampanchayat of Anurabad operates under the Taluka XYZ, District ABC in Maharashtra. We are responsible for delivering local governance, development work, sanitation, water supply, infrastructure, and rural welfare schemes. With this portal, we aim to increase accessibility and trust.
